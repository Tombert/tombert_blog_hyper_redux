I mentioned before that [[Posts/Personal/August 2025/I am a Master\| now have a masters degree]] in Computer Science.  

I enjoyed it, and it's nice to be able to say that I have a graduate degree now, but ultimately my biggest passion, outside of [video games with monkeys throwing barrels at alligators](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Donkey_Kong_Country_2), has always been mathematics. Ultimately it's the only thing I've ever been any good at, and I also think it's realistically the only place I could ever make significant contributions to the theory world. 

So I have started another masters degree at [The Open University](https://www.open.ac.uk/) in math, more on the pure side.  I'm currently taking Analytical Number Theory and Fractal Geometry.  It's been fun. 

Hopefully I finish it, and then I'll be exceptionally overqualified for a software engineering job.
